Indiana Jones

Raiders of the Lost Ark

Lucas conceived Raiders of the Lost Ark
in the early 1970's. Seeking to modernize
the serial films of the early 20th-century,
he developed the idea further with
Kaulfman, who suggested the Ark as the
film's goal. Lucas eventually focuse on
developing his 1977 space opera Star
Wars.

Temple of Doom

The film was released to financial
success but initial review were mixed,
criticizing its darker elements and
violence. However, critical opnion has
improved since 1984, citing the film's
intensity and imagination.

The Last Crusade

After the mixed reaction to Indiana Jones
and the Temple of Doom, Spielberg chose
to tone down the dark tone and graphic
violence in the next installment. During
the five years between the Temple of
Doom and The Last Crusade, he and
executive producer Lucas reviewed
several scripts before accepting Jeffrey
Boam's.
